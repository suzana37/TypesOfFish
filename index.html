FishZoneAbout

Fish is a member of paraphyletic group of organisms. This consists of gill-bearing aquatic craniates animals with limbs and digits. Most of the fishes are hagfish, cartilaginous, bony fish and lampreys. Fishes are ectothermic which means cold-blooded. Fish are abundant in most of the bodies of water. Fishes are an important resource for human in worldwide especially as food because it consists of a lot of minerals, vitamins and proteins as it stays in water bodies.
