European Seabass

It is classified under the higher classification of Dicentrarchus. These are primarily ocean-going fish. These are seen in brackish and fresh waters as well. It is also known as sea dance. These are marketed by loup de mer, robalo, spigola, brinzino, Mediterranean seabass and Branzino. It is regarded as table fish. It is mostly night hunter and feeds on small fish like crustaceans, cephalopods and polychaetes. Lifespan: It has a lifespan of about 25 years.
