Common Carp

It is classified under cyprinus. Body mass of this fish is about 2-14 kilograms. These are grown in freshwater lakes. Mostly found in water bodies in Asia and Europe. They can tolerate low oxygen levels. These are omnivorous. It can lay up to 300,000 eggs in a single spawn. This fish is taken as food by humans all over the world. Lifespan: Lifespan of common carp is until 47 years.
